About Us

Techbit Projects is a service-oriented company established in the Vijnan Institute of Science and Technology. We provide technical assistance and support in terms of electronics, hardware, and software. We organize and conduct technical sessions and workshops for students, professionals, and self-help groups to help them build their technical skills to endure in the future.
